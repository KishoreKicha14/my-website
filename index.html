About

Welcome to my personal website! I am Kishore Kumaar Natarajan, an AI researcher and data engineer with a strong focus on reinforcement learning and robotics. With a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence and three years of industry experience, I am passionate about developing intelligent systems that make a significant impact.

Currently, I am working at Nissan, where I specialize in creating deep learning models and integrating reinforcement learning algorithms for autonomous driving systems. My expertise includes working with sensor data, LiDAR, CAN, and image data to enhance decision-making processes and improve automation efficiency. I am proud to have achieved an 80% reduction in memory and runtime for mode transitions automation at Nissan.

Before joining Nissan, I worked as a Research Assistant at San Jose State University, where I developed a voice-controlled wearable robotic device that improved task performance by 30% in pilot tests. My journey began at Atlassian (Cognizant), where I implemented a customer attrition model, developed a recommendation engine, and built ETL pipelines, leveraging my skills in Python, SQL, Bash, and various data processing and visualization tools.

My technical skills encompass a wide range of programming languages and tools, including Python, SQL, Bash, Pandas, Numpy, PySpark, Power BI, Tableau, Keras, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, FastAPI, Postman, REST APIs, Pytest, Jenkins, Git, GitHub, AWS (Glue, S3, Redshift), and Azure (DevOps, ADF Orchestration, ADLS Gen 2, ADF, Databricks).

In addition to my professional work, I am dedicated to continuous learning and growth. I am currently reading 'Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction' and exploring Monte Carlo methods. I aspire to become an expert in R&D, particularly in reinforcement learning, and am eager to master new skills such as ROS (Robot Operating System) and computer vision with LiDAR.

I am also passionate about teaching and plan to introduce middle school students to the exciting world of AI through a structured program. My mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators and equip them with the skills they need to excel in the field of artificial intelligence.

Thank you for visiting my website. Feel free to explore my work and connect with me for collaborations or discussions on AI and robotics.
